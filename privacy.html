Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

1. Who We Are

Antakya Perfumes ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you visit our website or make a purchase.

If you have any questions, contact us at: hello@antakyaperfumes.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Billing and delivery address when you place an order.
  • Phone number for delivery notifications.
  • Payment details (processed securely by our payment provider — we do not store card details).
  • Communications you send us, including enquiries and feedback.

We may also automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version, operating system, referral source.
  • Pages viewed and time spent on each page (via analytics tools).
  • Cookie data (see Section 7).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your orders.
  • To communicate with you about your order status.
  • To send you marketing emails (only with your consent — you may unsubscribe at any time).
  • To improve our website and customer experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To prevent fraud and protect our business.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR)

  • Contract: Processing necessary to fulfil your orders.
  • Legitimate Interests: For fraud prevention, website security, and improving our services.
  • Consent: For marketing communications (which you can withdraw at any time).
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Delivery partners (e.g. Royal Mail, DPD) to fulfil your order.
  • Payment processors (e.g. Stripe, PayPal) to process transactions securely.
  • Analytics providers (e.g. Google Analytics) to understand website usage.
  • Legal authorities where required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy. Order records are retained for 7 years in line with HMRC requirements. You may request deletion of your account data at any time by contacting us.

7. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. These include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g. cart, session).
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site.
  • Marketing cookies: Used to show relevant advertisements (only with consent).

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@antakyaperfumes.com.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All transactions are encrypted using SSL/TLS technology.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on our website. Your continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
